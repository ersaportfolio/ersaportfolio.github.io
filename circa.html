CIRCA

  • Date

    05 December 2022

  • Services

    User Interface, Prototyping

  • Client

    SBI, SIG

CIRCA is an Indonesian electric motorbike company that inspired by the communities, thrilled by the adventures, and excites by good design. The brand founded in 2020 with 5 years Research and Development to bring different approach in riding with its quality in manufacturing and responsibility towards surroundings with clean electric energy.

In this CIRCA project, I was responsible for creating the mobile user interface that would be integrated with the motorcycle. Due to the tight timeframe, I began by conducting a small research, which involved benchmarking and desk research. Following this, I proceeded with sketching and establishing a design system. This design system was developed in parallel with the mobile design I was working on.
