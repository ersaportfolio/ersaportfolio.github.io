POLLEN

  • Internship Date

    May - July 2023

  • Services

    Research, User Interface, Testing

  • Company

    Pollen, Singapore

Pollen is a private B2B liquidation marketplace, committed to reducing global business waste by connecting sellers of excess inventory with global buyers. Customers benefit from sustainability and pricing analytics to find the best liquidation outcome that reduces usable products from being disposed of in landfills.

At Pollen, I supported the senior user experience team, which led to my involvement in various aspects of research. I created research documents based on competitor analysis. Additionally, I aided in constructing user flow and information architecture. I also contributed to the wireframing process and participated in testing and quality assurance for the developed designs. Furthermore, I assisted the product designer in transitioning from low-fidelity to high-fidelity designs and crafting new layouts or designs for specific cases.
