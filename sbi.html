Solusi Bangun Indonesia

  • Date

    22 June 2023

  • Services

    User Interface, Prototyping

  • Client

    SBI, SIG

PT Solusi Bangun Indonesia Tbk is a publicly traded Indonesian company where the majority of its shares (83.52%) are owned and managed by PT Semen Indonesia (Persero) Tbk (SIG) - the largest building materials manufacturer in Indonesia and Southeast Asia.

In the SBI project, I attempted to redesign the previously established company profile. Initially, I conducted benchmarking and desk research using the existing data from SBI. Afterward, I created sitemap, sketches and design system concurrently with the website design.
